#### Grammar

**吧 (ba)**

Used at the end of the sentence to make a suggestion (how about we...) or to indicate uncertainty in a claim (..., right?).

-

**再 + ...**

Indicates doing something again.

等一会儿 = to wait a little bit
再等一会儿 = wait a little bit *longer*
